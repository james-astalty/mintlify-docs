## Assigning and Reassigning a Task to another Team Member

We know that the lives of NDIS professionals are very busy and unpredictable. You may take a call for another coordinator or staff member for many reasons. That is why we have created the task assigning and reassign functionality within Astalty. This means that you can record a Task, delegate, and reassign the Task to anyone within your team or organisation, pending if you have the permission to do so.

<Info>
  **Handy Hint**

  This setting can be restricted in the 'users' section of Astalty. You can find more information about this in our [**Updating Roles and Permissions**](https://docs.astalty.com.au/guide/roles-and-permissions) article.

  The delegation or assigning of a Task during itâ€™s creation can be done by all users. Once the Task is created, it can only be reassigned by an Admin or Manager user.
</Info>

## Reassigning Tasks

To reassign Tasks you must first create a Task. From here you can click '**Actions**' and then '**Reassign Task**' as seen below.

![Assign And Reassign Task Image](/images/assign-and-reassign-task-image.png)

Upon selecting Reassign Task, you will see the following screen and will be prompted to select a user account to reassign the Task to.

![2 1717126232](/images/2-1717126232.png)

When you have selected the correct user, their name will show in the drop down box. Once you have selected the correct user for reassigning select '**Save**'.

![3 1717126243](/images/3-1717126243.png)

Once saved, you will receive the prompt 'The Task has been reassigned' as seen below.

![4 1717126257](/images/4-1717126257.png)

Now the task will populate on the reassigned users Task Board and they will be assigned to the Task as seen below.

![5 1717126280](/images/5-1717126280.png)

To reassign a Task, **all** of the following conditions must be met;

1. The Task does not have any time assigned to it.
2. The Task does not have any charges attached or all charges have a quantity of 0.
3. The Task is not marked as Complete.
4. You have permission to reassign Tasks.
5. The Task is not associated with a Calendar Event.

\
Users will also receive an email notification as well as a notification within Astalty ensuring that they will not miss if a Task has been assigned to them.

## Reassigning Tasks to another Participant

To do this, from the task view, simply click the Participant dropdown box and select the Participant you would like to reassign the Task to.

![Reassign Participant Task View](/images/reassign-participant-task-view.png)

This can also be done on the Task Board by clicking the Participants initials and selecting the new Participant from the dropdown box.

![Reassign Participant Task Board](/images/reassign-participant-task-board.png)
