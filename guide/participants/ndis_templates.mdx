## Overview

To streamline the process of accessing and utilising NDIS-related templates within participant profiles, follow the steps outlined below. This efficient approach not only saves time but also ensures accuracy by automatically filling in participant details on certain documents.

## Access Participant Profiles

- Navigate to the participant profile by clicking on the profile of interest.

## Locate Templates

- Within the participant's profile, find the templates section by clicking on 'Templates' in the left-hand side menu.

![Templates 11](/images/templates-11.png)

## Select Template Type

- At the top of the templates section, you can view and choose between custom templates and NDIA (National Disability Insurance Agency) templates.

![Ndia Template 11](/images/ndia-template-11.png)

## Explore NDIA Templates

- Under NDIA templates, you'll find various options including:
  - NDIS Consent Form
  - Request for a Review of a Decision
  - Change of Details or Situation
  - Application for a Replacement Support
  - Support Coordinator Progress Report
  - Recovery Coach Progress Report
  - Support Coordinator Implementation Report
  - Recovery Coach Implementation Report
  - Early Childhood Provider Report
  - Support Evidence Form - Home and Living
  - Conflict of Interest Declaration
  - Notify Participant Death

## Downloading Reports

- For progress and implementation reports, click 'Download' to obtain a prefilled report with the participant's details. This feature simplifies the form-filling process significantly.

## Configuring the NDIS Consent Form

To generate an NDIS consent form, select 'Generate'.

![Screen Shot 2024 03 11 At 1 12 21 Pm](/images/screen-shot-2024-03-11-at-1.12.21-pm.png)

Fill in the required information, such as:

![Screen Shot 2024 03 11 At 1 13 47 Pm 1710123594](/images/screen-shot-2024-03-11-at-1.13.47-pm-1710123594.png)

- Part B: Identify who will sign the consent form (participant or participant’s representative).
- Specify to whom the consent is being given (a person within your organisation or an external organisation).
- Select the information to be shared (choose 'All' or specific headings relevant to your organisation’s requirements).
- Determine the scope of the consent regarding changes to the participant’s personal details, communication preferences, and other consents on behalf of the participant.
- Specify the duration of the consent (one-time, ongoing, or until a specified date).

After completing the form, generate it to download a Word document with all the pre filled information.
