## Overview

The Utilisation page within a Participant's profile will give you a snapshot of the Participant's utilisation across their current Services.

The learn more about the Utilisation reporting please take a look on the [**Utilisation Report page**](https://docs.astalty.com.au/guide/reports/service-utilisation-report).

![Util2](/images/util2.png)

## Seeing the Utilisation of a previous plan period

You can also select the utilisation of a previous plan period by selecting the plan dropdown menu and select the relevant plan you would like to view the utilisation for.
