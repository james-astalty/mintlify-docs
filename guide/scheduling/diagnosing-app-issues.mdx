## Overview

If you experience issues with the Astalty app, providing clear and detailed information helps our support team diagnose and resolve the problem faster. This guide outlines the quick checks you can perform, the information you should collect, and how to submit a report.

## 1. Quick Checks

Before contacting support, try the following:

- **Hard close** the app and re-open it to see if the issue persists.
- **Check for updates:**
  - Go to the far-right screen (person icon).

![image1-1758494101.png](/images/image1-1758494101.png)

- Tap on Runtime Version

![image2-1758494204.jpg](/images/image2-1758494204.jpg)

- If an update is available, download the update.

## 2. Collecting Information

When reporting an issue, please include:

- A short **video recording** showing the exact action that caused the issue.
- The **edition and model** of your phone (e.g., _iPhone 14 Pro_, _Samsung Galaxy S22_).
