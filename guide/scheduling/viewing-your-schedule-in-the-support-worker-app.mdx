## Overview

The calendar in the Support Worker app allows you to view your upcoming schedule, see which days you have supports, and identify any incomplete shifts that need attention.

## Steps to View Your Calendar

- On the bottom menu of the app, tap the middle calendar icon.
- This will open your schedule view.

![image1-1757993006.png](/images/image1-1757993006.png)

- Use the arrows at the top of the page to toggle between weeks.

![image3-1757993034.png](/images/image3-1757993034.png)

- Select a specific day to view supports scheduled for that date.

![image2-1757993056.png](/images/image2-1757993056.png)

## Calendar Indicators

- The current day is highlighted with blue text.
- A dot beneath the date indicates that you have one or more supports scheduled on that day

## Changes to your Schedule - Push Notifications

There a different types of notifications that may be pushed to your Astalty App. You may receive notifications for changes to your schedule - Selecting these notifications will navigate you to your Schedule in your Astalty app to view these changes.

If the notification relates to a publishing of multiple shifts at once, you will receive a single message summarising the the shifts you've been assigned to.
