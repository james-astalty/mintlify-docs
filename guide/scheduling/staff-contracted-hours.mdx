## **Overview**

The Staff Contracted Hours feature allows you to record and manage each staff member’s employment details, including their start and end dates, employment basis, and contracted weekly hours. 

To update a staff member’s contracted hours, the user must have the **Manage HR Details for Users** permission enabled.

![image1-1757911157.png](/images/image1-1757911157.png)

For more information on permissions, click on the following [**link**](https://docs.astalty.com.au/guide/users/roles-permissions).

Once this permission is active:

- Open the **User Profile**.
- Click on the **HR** tab.
- Edit the **Contract Details**.

Details on this page include:

- **Start Date** and **End Date. **
- **Employment Basis** – Part-time, Full-time, or Casual. 
- **Weekly Hours** – Used to store contracted hours. 

![image2-1757911176.png](/images/image2-1757911176.png)

Weekly hours are useful for monitoring workload, on the **Schedule** page, you can see how many hours the support worker has worked in a given week.
