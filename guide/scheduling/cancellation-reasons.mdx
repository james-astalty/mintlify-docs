## Overview

In Astalty, you can create and manage your own cancellation reasons. These reasons will appear whenever a support is cancelled, ensuring that cancellations are tracked consistently and accurately across your organisation.

## Adding a Cancellation Reason

- Go to Settings.
- Select [**Scheduling**](https://app.astalty.com.au/settings/scheduling).

![image4-1757975112.png](/images/image4-1757975112.png)

- Click Cancellation Reasons.
- Enter the reason (e.g., Rostering Error).
- Click Create to save.

## Editing or Updating Reasons

To edit an existing cancellation reason:

- Click the three dots next to the reason.
- Select Edit.

![image1-1757975191.png](/images/image1-1757975191.png)

- Update the details as required.

## Using Cancellation Reasons

- Open the support you want to cancel.
- Select Actions â†’ Cancel Support.

![image3-1757975295.png](/images/image3-1757975295.png)

- Follow the prompts.
- From the dropdown list, select the appropriate cancellation reason.

![image2-1757975330.png](/images/image2-1757975330.png)

The cancellation will be recorded with the selected reason.
