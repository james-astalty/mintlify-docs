## Overview

Centre Capital Costs can be added to group supports or one-on-one supports that take place in a group setting (e.g., a centre). These costs must be enabled in the **Charge Item Set** before they can be applied to a support.

For more information on**Charge Item Sets,**  click here. 

## How to Add Centre Capital Costs

1. **Open the Scheduler**
   - Go to the **Scheduler** and click **Add Shift**.
2. **Select Group Support**
   - Choose **Group Support** at the top of the shift setup page.
   - Note: Centre Capital Costs can also be added to one-on-one supports taking place in a centre.
3. **Fill in Support Details**
   - Add the **location**, **support worker**, and **participants**.
4. **Enable Centre Capital Costs in the Charge Item Set**
   - Make sure the Charge Item Set used for this support has **Centre Capital Costs. **
   - Without this, you will not be able to apply Centre Capital Costs to the support.
5. **Apply Centre Capital Costs**
   - Scroll to the bottom of the shift setup page.
   - Under **Charge Options**, tick **This group support includes Centre Capital Costs**.

![image2-1757999628.png](/images/image2-1757999628.png)

6. **Save the Support**
   - Once selected, Centre Capital Costs will be billed automatically based on the total hours of support provided.
   - **Example:** For a 4-hour group support, 4 hours of Centre Capital Costs will be billed to **each participant**.

## Billing Options

- **Automatic Billing**\
  If enabled, Centre Capital Costs will be billed for the **entire duration** of the support (e.g., 4 hours of support = 4 hours billed to each participant).
- **Alternative Billing**\
  If you want to bill a different number of hours than the support provided (e.g., billing 2 hours of Centre Capital Costs for a 4-hour support), you can use the **Additional/Manual Charges** function.

## FAQs

**Will Centre Capital Costs automatically be added to a support?**\
No. They will only be added if you select the box **‘This group support includes Centre Capital Costs’.**

**Does Centre Capital Costs bill automatically based on support hours?**

Yes. When enabled, Centre Capital Costs are billed for the entire duration of the support, with the full cost applied to each participant.

**Can you add Centre Capital Costs to one-on-one supports?**\
Yes. Centre Capital Costs can be added to one-on-one supports delivered in a centre.

**What if I want to bill Centre Capital Costs differently from the scheduled hours?**\
You can use the **Manual Charges** functionality to add or adjust Centre Capital Costs independently of the support’s scheduled duration.
