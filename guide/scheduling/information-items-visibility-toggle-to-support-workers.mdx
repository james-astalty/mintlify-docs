## Overview

Information Items allow Astalty users to store important participant details under customisable headings. By enabling the Visible to Support Workers option, you can make specific Information Items available in the Astalty app for Support Workers to view on the participant’s profile. This feature is ideal for controlling which details are shared with Support Workers while keeping other information visible only to office staff.

## Turning On Visible to Support Workers

- In the left-hand menu, go to Settings.
- Select Participants.
- At the top of the screen, click Information Items.
- Locate the Information Item you want to update, click the three dots, and select Edit.

![image2-1757913743.png](/images/image2-1757913743.png)

- Tick the Visible to Support Workers checkbox.

![image1-1757913763.png](/images/image1-1757913763.png)

- Click Update to save your changes.

Once updated, the selected Information Item will be visible to Support Workers in the Astalty app under the participant’s profile.
